About me

Hello Friend,
Welcome to my page! I would like to introduce myself and personally thank you for stopping by. My name is Nathan and I am currently in Texas, originally from Michigan. My hobby of coding scripts for games started at a young age but I never thought it was possible to do what you loved and not just do something for the money. I never stopped being involved in one way or another with coding; Android Studio, Arduino and Raspberry pi are just a few examples of what helped to really open my eyes and start my journey into coding as a career. The fact that I can tell a computer what to do and it would do it without making a mistake, unless I made the mistake really intrigued me. Not only do I enjoy coding, I also enjoy; trail-riding with the quads, classical music, creative collaborative projects, DIY projects, history, reading, camping, binge-watching Hulu or Netflix, and working with my hands in my garage ...My garage is what my wife calls my "Laboratory", to me its more my sanctuary.
